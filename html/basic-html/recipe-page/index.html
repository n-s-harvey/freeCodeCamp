Delicious Cranberry Sauce

This is the cranberry sauce that I love to make for Thanksgiving. It is wonderful on its own or with some turkey!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
  • Optional: two cinnamon sticks

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve sugar in orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Stir in cranberries and continue heating. They will begin to pop. Cook for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Pour the sauce into a bowl or storage container. It will thicken as it cools.
